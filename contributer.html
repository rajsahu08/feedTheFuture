Food Rescue US is committed to ending hunger and reducing food waste by directly transferring fresh, usable food that would have otherwise been thrown away from grocers, restaurants, and other food industry sources to food insecure families throughout the U.S. Food Rescue US works with more than 2,700+ food donors, 17,000+ food rescuers, and 2,500+ social service agencies across 40 locations in 22 states and the District of Columbia, and we are constantly growing. Since our founding, we have reduced food waste by over 133+ million pounds and provided more than 105+ million high quality meals to the increasing number of people experiencing food insecurity. To make a donation by check, please make checks payable to Food Rescue US. All checks can be mailed to the following address: Food Rescue US, 1127 High Ridge Rd., Suite 338, Stamford, CT 06905 *Donations for our national efforts and individual Food Rescue US locations can be made through this page. There will be a question during the donation process for you to allocate your donation. **For donations allocated towards Food Rescue US locations, please note that 15% of the donations will be designated towards the general operations of Food Rescue US which includes essential technology, fundraising, marketing, and business support for all locations.

Despite the significant progress India has made, a large number of people still face the risk of food insecurity. The COVID-19 pandemic has further exacerbated the challenge of hunger in our country. As a response to the devastating consequences of the pandemic, Feeding India by Zomato launched the Daily Feeding Program in January 2021 to provide daily meal support to the most vulnerable families. Under this program, Feeding India has served 4.3+ crore meals with the main focus on reducing hunger for the most vulnerable. This has been enabled with the support of over 140 NGO partners spread across 40 cities in the country.
